#Slide: 2
#Header: Introduction to Shampoo
#Content: Shampoo is a cleansing product used for hair. It removes dirt, oil, and product buildup while maintaining hair health.

#Slide: 3
#Header: Ingredients Overview
#Content: Key ingredients include surfactants, thickeners, preservatives, and fragrances. Each plays a vital role in effectiveness and user experience.

#Slide: 4
#Header: Equipment Needed
#Content: Essential equipment includes mixing bowls, scales, stirrers, and storage containers. Proper tools ensure accurate measurements and mixing.

#Slide: 5
#Header: Preparation Steps
#Content: 1. Measure ingredients accurately.  
2. Mix surfactants with water.  
3. Add thickeners and other ingredients.  
4. Stir until homogenous.  
5. Bottle and label.

#Slide: 6
#Header: Tips for Formulation
#Content: Start with small batches. Test pH and viscosity. Adjust fragrance and color as desired. Document each variation for future reference.

#Slide: 7
#Header: Common Variations
#Content: Explore variations like moisturizing, clarifying, or natural shampoos. Each variation targets specific hair needs and preferences.

#Slide: 8
#Header: Safety Considerations
#Content: Always wear gloves and goggles. Follow guidelines for safe ingredient handling. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

#Slide: 9
#Header: summary
#Content: This presentation covered shampoo preparation, including ingredients, equipment, steps, and safety. Understanding these elements leads to effective formulations.
